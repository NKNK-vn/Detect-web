
Better Than Sex Cake Number 3
Category: Desserts:  Cake, cookies, mousse, puddings, etc..
Posted by:     Jamie Wernitznig (UseNet)
Quantity: 12 servings

Ingredients:
1 pkg. Yellow or white cake   1 c  Crushed pineapple,
mix                           undrained
1/2 c  Sugar                  1 pkg. Instant vanilla
                              pudding, 3 oz
1 c  Coconut                  8 oz Cool Whip; thawed
1 c  Pecans; toasted and      
chopped

Instructions:
Bake cake according to directions is a 9x13 pan. In a
saucepan over medium heat, combine pineapple with juice,
sugar, pudding, and coconut. Simmer 5 minutes. Using a
wooden spoon handle, poke holes in warm cake. Slowly pour
pineapple mixture over cake and spread evenly. Cool. Spread
whipped topping evenly over cake and sprinkle with nuts.
Chill at least 2 hours before serving.
