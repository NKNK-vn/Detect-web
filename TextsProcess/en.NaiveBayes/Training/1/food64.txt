Buffalo Wings
Category: Poultry:  Chicken, duck, goose, turkey, etc...
Posted by:     Magnolia
Quantity: ??

Ingredients:
A "bunch" of wing/drumstick-  Stick of butter
lets
Hot Sauce of your choice      

Instructions:
Fry the drumstick-lets/wings till done, in oil.  Remove and
drain. Melt butter in skillet, add desired amount of hot
sauce, about 1/4 to 1/2 cup, to butter and stir.  Add
wing/drumstick-lets to butter mixture to reheat and coat.
Dip in Roquefort dressing.
