Breads

1 Pound    1.5 Pound    
1C         1 1/2C    sour cream or yogurt, regular or low fat
1T         2T        canola oil
1C         1 1/2C    raisins
1t         1 1/2t    salt, to taste
1T         2T        dark brown sugar
1t         2t        cinnamon
1/2t       1t        ground cloves
2C         3C        unbleached all-purpose flour

Follow your bread machine manufacturer's instructions for
ingredient's order and yeast handling.  Bake on raisin bread cycle.
Put raisins in at the beep(s).

1 Pound    1.5 Pound    
1C         1 1/3C  applesauce, sweetened or unsweetened
2T         3T      unsalted butter or canola oil
1T         3T      honey
1/2C       1C      raisins
1/2C       1C      almonds or walnuts, coarsely chopped
1/4t       1/2t    cinnamon
1t         2t      salt, to taste
2C         3C      unbleached all-purpose flour
1 1/2t     2t      regular active dry yeast


Follow your bread machine manufacturer's instructions for
ingredient's order and yeast handling.  Bake on raisin bread cycle.
Put raisins in at the beep(s).


Light Whole Wheat Bread (Bread Machine)

1 Pound    1.5 Pound    
1C         1 1/2C    sour cream or yogurt, regular or low fat
1T         1 1/2T    canola oil
2T         3T        honey
1t         1 1/2t    salt, to taste
3/4C+1T    1 1/4C    water
1C         1 1/2C    whole wheat flour
1C+2T      1 2/3C    bread  flour
1 1/2t     2 1/4t    regular active dry yeast


Follow your bread machine manufacturer's instructions for
ingredient's order and yeast handling.  Bake on basic white bread
cycle.


Beer Bread (Bread Machine)

1 Pound    1.5 Pound    
1C         1 1/2C    flat beer
1T         2T        canola oil
2T         1/4C      sugar
2 1/4C     3 1/2C    unbleached all-purpose flour
1 1/2t     2t        regular active dry yeast


Follow your bread machine manufacturer's instructions for
ingredient's order and yeast handling.  Bake on basic white bread
cycle.



Garlic Herb Bread (Bread Machine)

1.5 Pound    
1C+1T     water
1/4C      olive oil
1/3C      nonfat dry milk
1t        salt
2 2/3C    unbleached all-purpose flour
2 1/2t    regular active dry yeast


Follow your bread machine manufacturer's instructions for
ingredient's order and yeast handling.  Bake on basic white bread
cycle.


Oatmeal Applesauce Bread (Bread Machine)

1 1/2T     butter or oil
1 1/2T     sugar
1t         salt
2t         apple pie spice
2T         nonfat dry milk
1/3C       quick cooking oatmeal
3C         unbleached all-purpose flour
2 1/2t     regular active dry yeast


Follow your bread machine manufacturer's instructions for
ingredient's order and yeast handling.  Bake on basic white bread
cycle.


Carrot, Raisin and Nut Bread (Bread Machine)

1 Pound      1.5 Pound    
1/4C         1/3C      water
1            1         egg
2T           3T        canola oil
1/4C         1/4C+2T   sugar
1t           1 1/2t    salt
1t           1 1/2t    ground cinnamon
1t           1 1/2t    ground ginger
1/2C         3/4C      rye flour
1 1/2C       2 1/4C    finely chopped or grated carrots
1 3/4C+1T    2 3/4C    bread flour
1/4C         1/2C      raisins
1/4C         1/2C      chopped walnuts

Follow your bread machine manufacturer's instructions for
ingredient's order and yeast handling.  Bake on raisin bread cycle.
Add raisins and nuts at the beep(s).
