Eric's Orange Frost:
Category: Beverages:  Drinks, punch, etc...
Posted by:     Chaster
Quantity:

Ingredients:
2 cups orange juice           6 ice cubes (regular big
                              ones)
1 scoop of orange or          
strawberry sherbet

Instructions:
mix all in a blender at high speed for about 30 seconds (or
until the ice has been reduced to a coarse slush)... Pour
into a tall   glass and serve immediately.  Makes about two
glasses.  Great stuff!   Yummmmmm....

Comments:
Just made up a tasty cold refresher for those hot summer
days (and nights!)
