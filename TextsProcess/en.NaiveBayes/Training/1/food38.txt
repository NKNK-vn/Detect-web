Elephant's Eye Berryetto
Category: Beverages:  Drinks, punch, etc...
Posted by:     Moe Mouse
Quantity:

Ingredients:
Several scoops of Ice Milk    1/2 to 1 cup strawberries,
                              frozen work best, whatever
                              your taste is
Creme de Cacao                Amaretto
(Ameretto)                    Crushed Ice

Instructions:
Blend all together and enjoy. I usually just kinda pour in
whatever amount of liquor I want, but not too much. It sound
awful, but it is wonderful!  Tell me if you like it!

Comments:
A really great ice cream drink I got from a bar in Kearney,
NE called the Elephant's Eye, The drink is called a
Berryetto.
